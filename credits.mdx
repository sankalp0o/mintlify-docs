---
title: "Credit usage"
description: "Understand what are credits and how they are used"
---

Running a document template both through the UI as well as the API costs credits from your account. Credit usage depends on various factors like number of pages in the document, text density on a page and advanced settings if you use them.

By default, new accounts have 500 free credits. To buy more credits, please reach out to us at contact@finigami.com. You can track credits available in your account from - [https://www.finigami.com/settings](https://www.finigami.com/settings).
